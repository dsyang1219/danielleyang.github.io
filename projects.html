Projects

Storm Shield

A weather-resistant headband designed to protect athletes' hearing aids and alert users to nearby hazards. The first prototype includes an acoustic hydrophobic mesh and an Passive Infrared Sensor alert system. This project was submitted to the Samsung Solve for Tomorrow competition, and state finalists wil be released in January.

Green Guide App

An app that provides localized recycling information and interactive activities to promote sustainable living. It was catered specifically for Bloomington youth, and submitted to the Nextech CS for Good competition.

Machine Learning for Real Time Classification of Transient Events: a Recurrent Neural Network Auto-Encoder and Gradient Boosting Classifier

a research paper on a machine learning method used to classify astronomical transients on unlabeled lightcurve data. This model worked with an AUC-ROC score of 0.94. It was accepted to the CCIR Nobel Laureate Symposium and has been submitted to the Regeneronn Science Talent Search.
